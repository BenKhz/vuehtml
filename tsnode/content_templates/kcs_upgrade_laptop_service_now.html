Having Trouble Upgrading Your Laptop?

Last updated September 2023

Problem: Can't Upgrade Laptop

You go to your profile to upgrade your laptop and see that you've met all of the requirements to upgrade, but you're still not able to.

Solution

Luckily, there are several options available to solve this issue. If you haven't met your requirements, then you'll need to either wait until the requirements are met, or you'll need to open a case for a special upgrade.

01

Open a Case

To open a case to investigate why you're unable to see your available upgrade, or to request a special upgrade, you'll simply need to go to your portal and click "Requests" and then "IT Services and Support". From there, you'll be able to submit your case and the IT Team will review and investigate.

02

Request a Catalog Item

You may be able to select the catalog item from your company's catalog and submit it as a new request and have it reconciled as an upgrade after the order is placed. Before you place the order, you should discuss this with a member of the IT Team or your Manager to see if this will be approved.

03

Wait Until Your Upgrade is Available

If your request for an early/special upgrade is denied, you may need to wait until you're eligible for the upgrade. If it's an emergent situation, you may be able to file an appeal. Discuss this with your manager first and they should be able to provide you with the information necessary to get your request approved.
