What is Global Day of Purpose?
Global Day of Purpose is a day dedicated to encouraging individuals and organizations to volunteer their time and resources to help their local communities.
Why is it Important for Our Company?
By participating in Global Day of Purpose, our company can demonstrate a commitment to giving back and help build strong relationships with the local community. It also provides an opportunity for our employees to come together and work on meaningful projects that can make a positive impact on those around us.

Benefits of Volunteering

How does volunteering benefit our company?

Volunteering helps build a positive image for our company and strengthens relationships with the community. It also provides an opportunity to bring employees together outside of their normal job roles and develop team building skills.

How does volunteering benefit our employees?

Volunteering empowers employees to make a difference outside of their job roles and gives them a sense of purpose. It also helps build self-confidence, develops team-building skills, and encourages them to think outside the box.

Volunteering Opportunities

What volunteering opportunities are available?

Our company has a wide range of volunteering opportunities available, from helping out at local charities to participating in community clean-ups. We also have opportunities for employees to mentor students or assist in fundraising efforts.

How can our employees get involved?

Employees can sign up for any of the available volunteering opportunities through our company's intranet portal. They can also join our volunteer network and receive updates on upcoming events or projects they can get involved in.

A Culture of Giving & Sharing

How can our company create a culture of giving and sharing?

Our company can create a culture of giving by encouraging employees to participate in volunteer programs, offering incentives for those who do, and recognizing their efforts through awards or recognition programs. We can also provide education on the importance of giving back and how it benefits both our employees and the community.

What can our employees do to create a positive culture of giving and sharing?

Employees can become active members of our volunteer network and help spread the word about upcoming events or projects. They can also help organize volunteer activities or serve as mentors for those in need. By leading by example, they can show their colleagues the importance of giving back and inspire them to become involved.

Supporting our Communities

What are the benefits of supporting our communities?

Supporting our communities helps build relationships with those around us and contributes to the overall health and well-being of our local area. It also provides an opportunity for employees to give back in meaningful ways and gain valuable insights into the needs of those we serve.

How can our employees become community ambassadors?

Employees can become community ambassadors by getting involved in local events or initiatives that benefit the community, such as food drives, clean-up days, or fundraisers. They can also use their skills, knowledge, and experience to mentor others or help with programs that promote education or job training.
