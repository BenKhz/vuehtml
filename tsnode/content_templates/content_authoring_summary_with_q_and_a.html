Onsite Child Care

Child care refers to the supervised care and nurturing of children, typically provided by caregivers or facilities outside of the child's home. It is essential for working parents, allowing them to fulfill their professional responsibilities while ensuring the well-being, safety, and development of their children. Child care options include daycare centers, preschools, after-school programs, and in-home care, catering to different age groups and needs. Effective child care not only supports parents' work commitments but also contributes to a child's early education, socialization, and overall growth.

What should I know?

Why is child care important?

Child care is crucial for working parents who needs a safe place for their children while they are at work. It also plays a significant role in children's early education, socialization, and cognitive development.
