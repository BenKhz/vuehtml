Frequently Asked Questions

Our wellness program encompasses a variety of health benefits, such as fitness facility access, nutritional guidance, and mental health resources, all aimed at enhancing overall well-being and empowering individuals to proactively manage their health.

How much do I pay for my benefits?

The cost of your benefits depends on the specific plan you choose and your coverage preferences, with options available to suit different budgets and needs.

When will my insurance start?

Your insurance coverage typically starts on the effective date specified in your policy, often coinciding with your employment start date or the date you become eligible for benefits.

Will I receive my ID cards?

Insurance ID cards are usually mailed to you shortly after your coverage begins, allowing you to have them on hand for medical appointments and other healthcare-related needs.

How do I order medication?

You can easily order your prescription medication through our online portal or app, where you can select your medications, provide your delivery address, and choose a convenient delivery option for a seamless and efficient process.

How do I change my address?

You can change your address by logging into your account and navigating to the "Profile" or "Settings" section, where you'll find an option to edit your address information.

How do I find a doctor?

You can search for in-network doctors by logging into our website or app and using the "Find a Doctor" tool, which allows you to filter by location, specialty, and other criteria to help you find the right healthcare provider for your needs.

How do I add a family member?

To add a family member, simply log into your account, navigate to the "Family Members" or "Dependents" section, and follow the prompts to provide their information and designate their coverage level.

How do I make changes to my benefits?

You can update your benefits by accessing your account during the open enrollment period or during qualifying life events, such as marriage or the birth of a child, by logging in and navigating to the "Benefits" or "Enrollment" section where you can modify your choices accordingly.
