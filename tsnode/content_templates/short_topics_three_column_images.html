Risk Management

The risk management

Risk management is the systematic process of identifying, evaluating, and addressing potential uncertainties to minimize adverse impacts and seize opportunities.

Operational risks

Operational risks encompass a range of potential disruptions and failures within an organization's processes, systems, and human resources that can impact its daily activities and objectives.

How to evaluate risks

Evaluating risks involves assessing their potential impact, likelihood of occurrence, and understanding the organization's risk tolerance to make informed decisions about risk mitigation strategies.
