Security Incident Response

Security incident response is a systematic approach to addressing and mitigating cybersecurity threats and breaches to ensure the integrity, confidentiality, and availability of digital assets and sensitive information.

Detection and Identification

Security incident response begins by identifying abnormal activities and potential breaches to swiftly address threats.

Containment and Eradication

Once identified, containment isolates the breach, while eradication ensures complete removal of the threat, minimizing further damage.

Recovery and Communication

Following containment and eradication, recovery restores systems, and open communication informs stakeholders about the incident's resolution and impact.

Managing Security Incidents

Managing security incidents involves promptly detecting, containing, and mitigating threats while communicating effectively to ensure a swift and effective response.
